Our Departments

Learn more about our various departments.

Build

Responsible for constructing the robot’s physical structure, assembling mechanisms, and ensuring everything is durable and competition‑ready.

Design

Creates CAD models, prototypes mechanisms, and ensures all robot components fit together efficiently before manufacturing.

Programming

Writes and maintains the robot’s code, including autonomous routines, tele‑op controls, sensors, and subsystem logic.

Electrical

Handles wiring, power distribution, sensors, CAN bus layout, and ensuring all electronics are safe and functional.

Strategy and Drive

Analyzes game rules, develops match strategy, and scouts other teams during competitions.

Marketing

Manages branding, outreach, social media, awards submissions, and team image to support sponsorship and community engagement.
