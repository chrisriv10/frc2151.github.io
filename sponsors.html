Thank You to Our Sponsors

We thank every sponsor, mentor, family, school partner, and community supporter who helps make FRC Team 2151 possible. Your support helps us buy robot parts, register for competitions, and give students hands-on experience with engineering, programming, design, marketing, and leadership.

The Monty Pythons are able to learn, compete, and represent Proviso Mathematics and Science Academy because people believe in our students. We are grateful for every donation, material contribution, and opportunity shared with the team.
